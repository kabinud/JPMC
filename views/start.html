Consent to automated identity verification (using facial recognition)

This demonstration involves the use of automated processing to verify your identity. In order to do this, we will collect certain personal information from you, including your selfie image and photo ID.

Specifically, to verify your identity, we will use facial recognition technology to check that the person in your selfie and your accepted photo ID are the same person. The faces in both photos are compared by creating a mathematical template based on measurement of various points on your face.

The templates created through this matching process are temporary and are not accessible to us. The templates are created and matched by a vendor on our behalf and an agent at the vendor will perform a manual review after the automated processing. All personal information collected and processed, including your selfie image and photo ID, will be deleted immediately after the processing is completed.
